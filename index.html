Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What is RO water purification?

+

Reverse Osmosis (RO) is a water purification process that removes contaminants, dissolved solids, and impurities from water by using pressure to force water molecules through a semi-permeable membrane, providing you with clean, safe drinking water.

How often should RO filters be replaced?

+

Filter replacement depends on usage and water quality. Typically, pre-filters should be replaced every 6-12 months, while RO membrane lasts 2-3 years. Our AMC plans include scheduled filter replacements to ensure optimal performance.

What is included in AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract)?

+

Our AMC includes regular maintenance visits, filter replacements, cleaning services, water quality testing, priority support, and emergency repairs. It ensures your system runs efficiently throughout the year.

Do you provide emergency repair services?

+

Yes! We offer 24x7 emergency support. Our team is available round-the-clock to address any urgent issues with your water purification system.

What areas do you serve?

+

We serve Tirupati and surrounding areas including Tiruchanur, Renigunta, and nearby regions. Contact us to confirm service availability in your area.

How do I choose the right RO system for my home?

+

Our expert consultants will assess your water quality, family size, and requirements to recommend the perfect system. We offer free water testing and consultation to help you make the right choice.
